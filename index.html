About Me

My college years as a Computer Science student,

I graduated from Technological University of the Philippines back in 2009 with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Computing major in Computer Science. I had my internship at IVANT Technologies where we created software and website applications to different companies. However, I was not able to pursue on my career as a computer programmer and took a different path with a BPO company.

My career outside of the Information Technology industry,

I worked with Accenture, Inc., for five years where I was able to highlight my skills, meet different people with different personalities and accomplish every task assigned to me professionally, objectively and with open-mindedness. I became a trainer and was even sent to India to train people there as a part of the expansion of our department. I then became a team leader and a mentor for teams from Philippines and India. My experience with Accenture, Inc. had been truly fruitful, wide and varied.
